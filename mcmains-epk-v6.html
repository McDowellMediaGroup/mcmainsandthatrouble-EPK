// The Band

The Story

Fort Worth, Texas-based rock band McMains & Tha Trouble blend high-energy Texas swagger with a sound that refuses to be pinned down or played safe.

Expect an unforgettable show that'll have you dancing, singing along, and headbanging — all at the same time.

With Frank's gritty vocals, Zach's 80s-shredding guitar work, and the powerhouse rhythm section of Johnny and Chris — drawing from grunge, pop-punk, and red dirt in equal measure.

Three Spotify releases in. Countless live shows. Opening slots for heroes like Cody Canada. This is the sound of Fort Worth at midnight. 🔥
